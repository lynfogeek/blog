I am co-founder and Android developer at Impraise. I am playing with the Android ecosystem since 2012, having fun developing apps & small games for mobiles and tablets and always willing to test the newest libraries and SDKs!

This blog will mainly focus on Android, from design and user experience to small tutorials, as well as talking about various open source project I build, contribute to, or enjoy using.

Originally from Lyon, I studied in France at Epitech where I learned various technologies (C, C++, web-based languages, kernel programming, etc) and worked for a web agency and a wind-farm company. In 2012, I spent one year in the lovely city of Jönköping, Sweden. I followed there the Master classes “Information Engineering and Management” and I never really came back home. After few months in Berlin and a trimester in the Bay area, I now live in Amsterdam since October 2014.

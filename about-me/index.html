I am co-founder and a senior engineer at Impraise. I strive to build products that have a positive impact on their users, and to overcome countless of challenges with my team.

This blog contains articles related to mobile development (which was my primary focus between 2012-2016), talks that I have given in meetup or conferences, and it may expand to people management, technical leadership, and backend / Elixir development.

Originally from Lyon, I studied in France at Epitech where I learned various technologies (C, C++, web-based languages, kernel programming, etc) and worked for a web agency and a wind-farm company. In 2012, I spent one year abroad in the city of Jönköping, Sweden. I followed there the Master classes “Information Engineering and Management” and I never really came back home. After few months in Berlin and a summer in the Bay area, I now live in Amsterdam since October 2014.
